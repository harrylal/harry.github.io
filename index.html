07 — How I work

Philosophy

01

Perception in the Physical World

The hardest problems in AI aren't solved in notebooks — they're solved when models perceive and act through real cameras, radars, and vehicles in unpredictable conditions.

02

Reliability Before Complexity

A simple system that works 99.9% of the time beats an elegant system that fails at the edge case. Engineering safety means engineering for failure modes.

03

Practical Innovation

Research matters when it ships. From the lab bench to NHR03 in production, I focus on techniques that translate from papers to the road — measurable impact over theoretical elegance.

04

Human-Centered Autonomy

Driver assistance should protect lives without overwhelming the driver. Safety, transparency, and trust are design requirements, not afterthoughts.
